Physics

The initial work of the team was in designing and refining our Barnes-Hut based algorithm and experimenting with other varieties of algorithm such as FMM. Our primary task afterwards was checking the accuracy of the GPU based simulation and assisting in the refinement of said simulation. This was achieved using a checker program based on our prototype code, designed to calculate N frames of a dataset at a higher accuracy than the GPU based simulation and then compare the two output sets. Thanks to our work we were able to achieve a marginal loss of accuracy. We also designed code to be run for determining performance metrics.
